# Style Guide: One Piece (Example)
>
> **IP**: One Piece / Большой Куш (Often kept as One Piece)
> **Target**: Russian (RU)

## Tone & Voice

- **Core Tone**: Adventurous, Wacky, Grandiose, "Romance Dawn".
- **Ratio**: 50% Character-heavy / 50% Official.
- **Keywords**: Nakama, Devil Fruit, Haki, Pirates.

## Terminology Policy

- **Names**: Transliteration.
  - Luffy -> Луффи
  - Zoro -> Зоро
- **Devil Fruits**: Translate pattern "Gomu Gomu no..."
  - Gomu Gomu no Pistol -> Гому-Гому но Пистолет (Hybrid) or Резиновый Пистолет (Translated). *Policy: Hybrid for attack names.*
- **Marines**: Дозорные (Watchers) or Морской Дозор.

## Grammar & Mechanics

- **Register**:
  - Pirate speech: Rough, informal `ты`.
  - System: Neutral `вы`.
- **Exclamations**: Use manga-style onomatopoeia where appropriate (Don!! -> Дон!!).

## UI Length & Brevity

- **Constraint**: Console/PC interface. Moderate expansion allowed (up to 30%).
- **Formatting**: Use colors `<color=#FF0000>...</color>` for key terms if supported.

## Forbidden Patterns

- No "One Piece" translation in text body unless referring to the treasure "One Piece" -> "**Ван Пис**". Title remains English.
- No "Scurvy dogs" literal translation unless context fits.

## Placeholder Handling

- **Order**: Subject-Object orders can swap in RU, but keep placeholder index `{0} {1}` intact.
- **Tags**: Respect HTML-like tags `<br>`.
