# Style Guide: Bleach (Example)
>
> **IP**: Bleach / Блич
> **Target**: Russian (RU)

## Tone & Voice

- **Core Tone**: Stylish, Poetic, Urban Fantasy, Cool, "Poems".
- **Ratio**: 80% Stylized / 20% Official.
- **Keywords**: Shinigami, Hollow, Bankai, Reiatsu.

## Terminology Policy

- **Concepts**: Keep Japanese terms transliterated.
  - Shinigami -> Синигами (Not "Бог Смерти")
  - Bankai -> Банкай
  - Zanpakuto -> Дзанпакто
- **Names**: Polivanov strictly? Fandom prefers "Ichigo -> Ичиго".
  - Policy: Use established fandom spellings for main cast, Polivanov for rest.

## Grammar & Mechanics

- **Register**:
  - Captains to subordinates: ARROGANT `ты` or commanding tone.
  - Subordinates to Captains: Respectful `Капитан ...`
- **Style**: Short, punchy sentences. "Poetic" breaks.

## UI Length & Brevity

- **Constraint**: Stylized UI with vertical text support? If horizontal, keep extremely short.
- **Menu Items**: English often kept in Bleach UI. If RU, single word.

## Forbidden Patterns

- Avoid over-explaining terms. "Bankai" needs no footnote.
- No hyphenation of "Rei-atsu" -> "Рейацу".

## Placeholder Handling

- Ensure placeholders representing names are treated as Nominative case by default.
